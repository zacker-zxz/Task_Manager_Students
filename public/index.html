Why NexusTask?

Everything a student needs to stay on top of deadlines.

Add & Organize

Create tasks instantly and sort them by category — assignments, exams, or personal.

Track Deadlines

Set deadlines for every task. Tasks are auto-sorted so urgent ones show first.

Mark Complete

Check off finished tasks. Your dashboard stats update live as you make progress.

Responsive Design

Works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops. Study anywhere.

Server Database

Tasks are saved on the Node.js backend so they persist across sessions and devices.

Login System

Register and log in with your own account. Your tasks are private to you.
